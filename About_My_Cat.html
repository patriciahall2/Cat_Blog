Meet Eva The Cat

Eva is a 13 year old Siamese cat. She is very affectionate and meows a lot which is common for her breed.

She is a rescue cat that has hyperthyroidism. She has to take medication twice a day with her meals.

She is on a raw food diet and gets 2 meat nuggets per meal. She eats twice a day. Once in the morning and once at night.

She needs to take 1.5 pills with each meal.

To give her her pills, open her mouth and put them in the back of her throat. Close her mouth then massage her throat to make her swallow.

Make sure she doesn't spit them out. Once she's swallowed her pills allow her to eat her meal.

She loves to play with strings, loves to be pet very gently, and loves to sleep with people. Her purrs are very relaxing to listen to.

She is allowed to go outside when the weather isn't too cold or rainy. She prefers relieving herself outside to using her litter box, although she is good about not peeing or pooping in the house.

She is does not get along with other cats or dogs, but loves people.

She is up to date on her shots.
